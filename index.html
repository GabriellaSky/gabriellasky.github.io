• Gabriella Skitalinskaya, M.Sc.
Hi, I'm

Gabriella Skitalinskaya

I am a PhD candidate in the Computer Science department at the University of Bremen advised by Henning Wachsmuth and Andreas Breiter.

I work on problems related to Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. In particular, I am interested in exploring applications of NLP such as understanding quality characteristics of arguments and argument generation.
