Hi, I'm

Gabriella Skitalinskaya

I am a researcher at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence of the Leibniz University Hannover. I work on problems related to Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. In particular, I am interested in exploring applications of NLP such as understanding quality characteristics of arguments and argument generation.
